The Join Step: Prefix Matching

How do we turn pairs (2-itemsets) into triplets (3-itemsets)?

The "Common Prefix" Rule

To form a candidate of size k, we take two itemsets of size k-1.
They must share the same first k-2 items (The Prefix). We merge them and attach the differing last items.

  • Example: {A, B} and {A, C} share prefix 'A'. Result: {A, B, C}.
  • Counter-Example: {A, B} and {D, E} have different prefixes. No merge.
