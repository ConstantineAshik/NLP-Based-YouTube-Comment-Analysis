Theory: From Trees to Rules

Decision Trees and Rule-Based Classifiers are closely related, but they interpret space differently.

  • Decision Tree: Uses a "Divide and Conquer" strategy. It cuts the whole space into smaller pieces until pure. Every point belongs to exactly one leaf.
  • Sequential Covering (Rule Lists): Uses a "Separate and Conquer" strategy. It finds one rule that covers a chunk of "easy" points, removes them (peels them off), and then looks at what is left.
