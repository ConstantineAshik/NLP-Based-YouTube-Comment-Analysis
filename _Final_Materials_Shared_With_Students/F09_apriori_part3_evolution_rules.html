2. The Pruning Filter (Apriori Property)

"If a subset is infrequent, the superset CANNOT be frequent."

Ready
Optimization: Before we scan the huge database for {A, B, C, D}, we check its subsets.
If we find that subset {B, C, D} was NOT in our frequent list (L3), we immediately TRASH the candidate.
